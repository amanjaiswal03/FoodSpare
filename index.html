    The Problem
  • Food banks and social institutions can't cope with the problem alone - the scale of the waste is too big
  • The supply chain from food industry businesses to the social organizations is far from optimal
  • Low awareness among businesses in the food industry on the tools and strategies they can employ to reduce food waste.
  • Lack of centralized systems to track the amount of food wasted and donated
    •
    The Solution
  • FoodSpare provides a platform to assist businesses in their initiatives to reduce food waste
    • Food businesses use the FoodSpare service to find local food banks and charities, as well as to track the number of donations and to check existing donation commitments.
  • FoodSpare provides a convenient way to gather data and to monitor the food distribution
  • FoodSpare connects businesses with social institutions, food banks and charities
